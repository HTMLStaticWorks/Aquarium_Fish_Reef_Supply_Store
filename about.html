Our Story

Crafting Liquid Masterpieces

We believe an aquarium is more than glass and water—it's a living, breathing work of art.

Born from Passion

Founded in 2010 by a team of marine biologists and aquascape artists, Aquaria started as a small local frag tank and grew into a premier destination for reef enthusiasts.

Our mission is to provide sustainably sourced, extremely healthy livestock, alongside the highest quality equipment, while educating hobbyists on the intricate biology of reef ecosystems. We quarantine every fish and dip every coral before it ever sees our display tanks.
